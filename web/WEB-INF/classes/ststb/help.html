I purchased a book but have not received it. What should I do?

Contact the seller and request the shipping date. Shipping methods like USPS Media Mailk while inexpensive, are slow. Deliver time can take many days to a couple week. Therefore, confirm that the expected arrival date has indeed passed.

It has been weeks since I purchased a book and I still have not received it. The seller is not communicating with me or, refuses to refund my purchase. What can I do?

Contact Paypal or your credit card company and ask them to refund your money and/or reverse the transaction.
